TikTok Content Posting API Flow

This page demonstrates the intended end-to-end integration flow for submitting original Griot Reels video episodes to TikTok.

Prepare Episode

An original Griot Reels vertical video episode is created for the brand.

Add Details

The title, caption, series name, and destination account are confirmed.

Upload Draft

The app sends the video to TikTok as a draft using the Content Posting API.

Review & Publish

The video is reviewed and published on the official @GriotReels TikTok account.

Data Use and Privacy

Griot Reels does not collect, store, sell, or share TikTok user data. The TikTok API is used only to upload original Griot Reels video content to our own official TikTok account. We do not access followers, comments, messages, or private user information.
